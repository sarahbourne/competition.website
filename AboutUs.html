Our mission:

To save you time and money on going out and drinking in Bath

Who we are:

We are a group of students living in Bath wanting to maximising the fun we can have at bars with limited funds! So we had the idea of a website to help us ‘go out’ efficiently: ‘Bars in Bath’!

What we do:

We have created a website with details of all the bars in Bath including their daily deals, drinks prices and location. You can view deals by the bar location; opening days and times; and drink types.

We find prices for each drink in every bar and display it in an easy way for anyone to understand and choose where to go out in Bath: select a day, drink or area and choose where to go based on best deals!

A work in progress:

Being a new start-up, we have the base idea for our website but also have plenty of plans and ideas for future growth.

We would like create an app, making it even easier to view bars and locations on the go.

We will also create a log in page for bars themselves to update their own information and advertise offers and deals.

We will also create a log in page for you – our customers. We will give you the ability to log on and have your own homepage. You will then have access to your own barcode which you can scan, from your phone, in bars, hopefully for a discount or a loyalty scheme… (keep your eyes peeled)
